I currently work on the interface of atmospheric mechanisms and physicochemical properties of atmospheric aerosols. I have interests in the mechanisms of secondary aerosol formation, aerosol aging processes, and radiative impacts of aerosols on the climate.

In particular, I have been investigating atmospheric processes that govern the dynamics of aerosols in the vertical profile of the lower Amazonian troposphere. I have experience in aerosol instrumentation and atmospheric data analysis.
